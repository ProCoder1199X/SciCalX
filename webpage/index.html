SciCalX v1.0 Alpha

Your go-to scientific calculator for physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics.

Key Features

  • Scientific Calculator: Perform basic and advanced calculations. Supports mathematical operations including addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and more.
  • Unit Converter: Convert between various units in categories like distance, weight, temperature, and more (30+ units supported!).
  • Fundamental Equations: Pre-loaded physics, chemistry, biology, and math equations for quick reference and calculations.
  • Theme Toggle: Switch between a Dark and Light theme to suit your preference.
  • MathJax Integration: For displaying complex equations.
  • Windows Support: Fully functional on Windows (with an easy-to-install .exe).
  • Scientific Notation Converter: To convert big numbers (eg. 300000) to scientific representation (3x10^8).
  • Alpha Version: With continuous updates to come!
